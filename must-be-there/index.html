What we encourage?

‘I must be there’ ushers invigorating trips for the ever-youthful club of 18-35 years of age. We believe that adventure and ecstatic outdoor activities are what keep the youth-young and heartened. Hence, we lay-out a holiday full of fun and fast-paced experiences to give you a time of your life you will hang on to forever.

Split-second posse

Group vacations feel like home away from home, where you can feel secure and care-free at all times. Not to mention, having the captain take care of all the hassled planning so that ‘fun’ is all you got to do!

Worthwhile experiences

Light on frills and heavy in experiences- ‘I must be there ’ makes even the elusive extravagant places possible to visit, along with the exciting activities at extremely affordable packages.

An introvert-free zone

‘I must be there’ tours free-spirited and ticks every column of your go-to lists. So come around with a boundless thirst of life, an Open mind and the best pair of your dancing shoes; to keep away the introvert side of you.
